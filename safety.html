Your Safety is Our Priority

Rydo is committed to making every ride safe, reliable and secure for both riders and drivers.

Safety Features

All Rydo drivers go through identity verification and safety training.

Live Ride Tracking

Share your live trip location with friends and family in real time.

SOS Emergency Button

Quickly contact emergency services and Rydo support during a ride.

Privacy Protection

Night Ride Safety

Additional safety monitoring for late-night rides.

24/7 Support

Our support team is available anytime to assist you.

Safety Tips for Riders

  • ✔ Verify the vehicle number before starting your ride.
  • ✔ Share your trip with trusted contacts.
  • ✔ Wear helmet for bike rides.
  • ✔ Use the SOS button in case of emergency.
  • ✔ Always follow traffic and safety guidelines.
