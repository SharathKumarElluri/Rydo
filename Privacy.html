Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is our responsibility.

For Users (Customers)

We collect personal information such as your full name, mobile number, and email address when you register or use the Rydo app. This information helps us create and manage your account securely. In addition, we collect real-time location data during ride requests and trips to ensure accurate pickup and drop services. We also gather payment-related details such as transaction history and payment method preferences. We may also collect device information including your IP address, browser type, device model, and operating system. This helps us analyze app performance, detect technical issues, and improve user experience. All collected information is used responsibly and only for the purposes outlined in this policy.
Your data is primarily used to provide seamless ride booking services. This includes matching you with nearby drivers, confirming bookings, and ensuring smooth communication between you and the driver. We also use your information to process payments securely, generate invoices, and maintain transaction records. Your data helps us improve our services by analyzing user behavior and identifying areas for enhancement. Additionally, we may use your contact details to send important notifications such as ride updates, safety alerts, and customer support responses. Promotional messages may also be sent, but only with your consent. Our goal is to use your data to deliver a safe, reliable, and personalized experience.
Location tracking is an essential part of the Rydo service. We collect your real-time GPS location to connect you with nearby drivers quickly and efficiently. During an active ride, your location is continuously tracked to ensure accurate navigation, route optimization, and safety monitoring. This also allows features such as live trip tracking and sharing ride details with trusted contacts. In some cases, location data may be collected even when the app is running in the background during a trip to ensure uninterrupted service. We ensure that your location data is handled securely and is not used for any unauthorized purposes.
We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. This includes encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and continuous monitoring systems. Your personal and financial data is stored in a secure environment to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. We regularly update our systems and conduct security audits to identify and fix potential vulnerabilities. Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data, and strict policies are followed to ensure confidentiality. Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure, but we continuously strive to maintain the highest level of protection.
As a user, you have full control over your personal data. You have the right to access the information we hold about you and request corrections if any details are inaccurate. You can also request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements. Additionally, you have the right to withdraw consent for certain data processing activities. We provide easy options within the app or through customer support to help you exercise these rights. Our aim is to ensure transparency and give you complete control over your information.

For Drivers (Partners)

We collect detailed information from drivers to ensure safety and authenticity on the platform. This includes your full name, phone number, driving license details, vehicle registration, and identity verification documents. These details are required to verify your identity and ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. We may also collect profile photos and background verification data where applicable. This information helps us maintain a trusted and secure environment for both drivers and users.
Driver data is used to manage ride allocations, track performance, and ensure smooth operations on the platform. This includes assigning rides based on location and availability. We also use this data to monitor service quality through ratings, feedback, and ride history. This helps us improve the overall experience for both drivers and users. Your information is also used for communication, support, and resolving any disputes that may arise during rides.
We collect and process your payment details, including bank account information, to ensure accurate and timely payouts. All transactions are securely recorded, and earnings are calculated based on completed rides, incentives, and applicable bonuses. Detailed earning summaries may be provided within the app. We use secure payment gateways and encryption technologies to protect your financial information at all times.
Location tracking is essential for drivers to receive ride requests and navigate efficiently. We collect real-time GPS data to match you with nearby users and provide route guidance. Your location is also tracked during trips to ensure safety, monitor ride progress, and handle disputes if necessary. This data helps improve efficiency, reduce wait times, and provide a better overall experience.
We ensure that all driver data is securely stored and handled in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Security measures such as encryption and access control are implemented to protect sensitive information. We may conduct regular checks and audits to maintain compliance and prevent misuse of data. Drivers can also request access or updates to their information and contact support for any privacy-related concerns.
