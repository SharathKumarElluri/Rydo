Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is our responsibility.

Rydo is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your information is handled. We value the trust you place in us and strive to maintain the highest standards of data protection while delivering a safe and seamless riding experience. This policy outlines how your data is collected, processed, stored, and safeguarded when you use our services.

We collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, real-time location data, payment details, and device information. This may also include app usage patterns, IP address, browser type, and interaction data to help us understand user behavior and improve our services.

Your data is used to provide ride services, match you with drivers, process payments, improve our platform, and ensure safety and fraud prevention. Additionally, we may use your information to personalize your experience, send important updates, provide customer support, and enhance service reliability.

Rydo uses GPS and location tracking to provide accurate ride matching, route optimization, and safety monitoring during trips. Location data may be collected even when the app is running in the background during an active ride to ensure continuous tracking and user safety.

We may share your data with drivers, payment gateways, customer support, and legal authorities when required. This sharing is strictly limited to necessary operations such as completing rides, processing payments, and complying with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We implement encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to protect your data from unauthorized access and breaches. Regular security audits and monitoring mechanisms are in place to detect and prevent vulnerabilities, ensuring your information remains protected at all times.

We retain your information only as long as necessary for providing services, complying with legal obligations, and resolving disputes. Once the data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with applicable laws and best practices.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data, withdraw consent, and request a copy of your personal information. We provide easy-to-use options within the app or through support channels to help you exercise these rights effectively.

We use cookies and analytics tools to enhance user experience, understand behavior, and improve our services. These technologies help us remember your preferences, optimize performance, and deliver a more personalized experience.

Our app may integrate with third-party services such as payment gateways and maps. These providers operate independently and have their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them for better understanding.

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or service improvements. Any updates will be communicated via the app or website, and continued use of our services implies acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@rydo.com. Our support team will assist you in resolving any issues related to your data and privacy.
